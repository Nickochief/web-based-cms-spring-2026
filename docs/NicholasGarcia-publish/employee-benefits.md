## Employee Benefits

Acme Corp offers a range of benefits to support your health and well-being.

### Health Insurance

Acme Corp provides health insurance coverage for all full-time employees. Contact HR at <sysadmin@acme.com> to enroll.

### 401k

Acme Corp matches up to 5% of your salary in your 401k plan. You are eligible to enroll after 90 days of employment.
