## Code of Conduct

Acme Corp is committed to maintaining a respectful and professional workplace for all employees. This page outlines the behavioral standards expected of every team member.

### Workplace Behavior

All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect. Follow these guidelines in all work settings:

- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism.
- Communicate clearly and professionally in all work settings.
- Report any misconduct or harassment to HR immediately at <sysadmin@acme.com>.

### Confidentiality

As an Acme Corp employee, you are responsible for protecting company and client information. Follow these guidelines:

- Do not share internal company information outside of Acme Corp.
- Keep all client data private and secure.
- If you are unsure whether information is confidential, contact your manager before sharing.
