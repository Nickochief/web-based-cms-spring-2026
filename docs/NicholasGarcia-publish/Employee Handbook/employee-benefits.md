## Employee Benefits

Acme Corp offers a comprehensive benefits package to support your health, financial well-being, and work-life balance. This page covers the key benefits available to all full-time employees.

### Health Insurance

Acme Corp provides health insurance coverage for all full-time employees starting on your first day. Your plan includes medical, dental, and vision coverage. To enroll or make changes to your plan, contact HR at <sysadmin@acme.com>.

### 401k Retirement Plan

Acme Corp matches up to 5% of your salary in your 401k retirement plan. You are eligible to enroll after 90 days of employment. To get started, log in to the employee portal or contact HR at <sysadmin@acme.com>.

### Time Off

Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. Submit time off requests to your manager at least two weeks in advance. See the HR Policies page for more details.
